***************************************************************
Example 3 - XD with Long-Blue + 10 l/mm grating - Point Source
***************************************************************

In this example, we will reduce the GNIRS crossed-dispersed observation of
a bright HD star.

This observation uses the 10 l/mm grating, the long-blue camera and the 0.10
arcsec slit.   The dither pattern is the standard ABBA, repeated 3 times.

The data were originally published in Bertoldo-Coelho  et al. 2024, MNRAS 530, 3651.
